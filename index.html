Quantitative Science Consulting

Welcome to Quantitative Science Consulting (QSC). We are a small data science consulting company specializing in statistics, machine learning, and ecological modeling. Dr. Mikey Tabak, our principal data scientist, has over 12 years of experience in developing quantitative methods to provide environmental solutions.
We work with expert software engineers to provide front-end software that deploys complex models on any operating system (including Mac, Windows, and mobile devices).

Services

QSC offers a variety of environmental data science consulting services including:
  • Automatically detecting, classifying, and counting animals in images (camera traps or aerial images)
  • Ecological modeling: population viability analysis, meta-population and meta-community modeling, populaiton dynamics simulations
  • Epidimiological modeling

Techniques

QSC uses state-of-the-art technologies:
  • Deep learning and computer vision
  • Hierarchical Bayesian modeling
  • High-performance computing
