Quantitative Science Consulting (QSC)

Welcome to QSC! We are a small data science consulting company taking projects in statistics, machine learning, and ecological modeling. Dr. Mikey Tabak, our principal data scientist has over 15 years of experience in developing quantitative methods to provide environmental solutions. We work with expert software engineers to provide front-end software that deploys complex models on any operating system (including Mac, Windows, and mobile devices).

Services

QSC offers a variety of environmental data science consulting services including
  • Automatically detecting, classifying, and counting animals in images (camera traps or aerial images)
  • Ecological modeling: population viability analysis, meta-population and meta-community modeling, populaiton dynamics simulations
  • Epidimiological modeling
