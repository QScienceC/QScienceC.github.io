Quantitative Science Consulting (QSC)

Welcome to QSC! We are a small data science consulting company specializing in statistics, machine learning, and ecological modeling. Dr. Mikey Tabak, our principal data scientist has over 15 years of experience in developing quantitative methods to provide environmental solutions.
We work with expert software engineers to provide front-end software that deploys complex models on any operating system (including Mac, Windows, and mobile devices).
