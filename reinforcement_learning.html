What is Reinforcement Learning?

Reinforcement learning (RL) is a machine learning approach that develops optimal strategies through trial-and-error. It allows an agent to interact with a dynamic environment, learning from rewards and penalties to make better decisions over time. This is especially valuable for manufacturing settings where conditions change and complex trade-offs must be made.

  • Agents are trained to explore and act in dynamic environments
  • Feedback is used to improve future decisions
  • Goals are achieved by maximizing long-term rewards

Optimizing Manufacturing Workflows

RL helps manufacturers optimize production schedules, inventory flows, energy usage, and more. Our systems learn from large-scale process data to automate smart decisions that reduce costs, minimize waste, and improve quality — with ongoing learning as operations evolve. See an example here.

We use reinforcement learning to reduce workplace accidents and support environmental goals. RL-powered systems proactively detect risk scenarios and trigger adaptive responses, helping to prevent incidents and lower emissions.
