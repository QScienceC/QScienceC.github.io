QSC Mobile Detect Usage Guide

Follow these steps to launch the Mobile Detect app, install it on your device, and keep it working offline.

Open Mobile Detect Need help?

Quick start

  1. Open qsc.earth/mobile-detect on your mobile device.
  2. Allow camera access so the app can display the live video feed.
  3. Point the camera at nearby objects and review the bounding boxes, labels, and confidence scores.
  4. Keep your device steady and aim for well-lit scenes to improve detection accuracy.

Install the app (recommended)

Installing the PWA keeps it on your home screen and makes it faster to launch.

  • iOS: Tap Share â†’ Add to Home Screen.
  • Android: Accept the install prompt or open the browser menu and choose Add to Home screen.

Offline use

  1. Open the app once while online so it can cache the model.
  2. After the first successful load, the app and model are stored locally.
  3. Launch the app anytime without a network connection to keep using it.

If you open the app offline before the model is cached, you will be prompted to reconnect.

Troubleshooting

  • Camera permission denied: Re-enable camera access for the site in your browser or OS settings.
  • No detections: Lower the confidence threshold in the app to show more results.
  • Blank screen: Force close the app or refresh the page, then try again.
  • Still having issues: Clear site data in your browser settings and reopen the app to re-download the model.
