Geospatial Analyses

For environmental organizations, planners, and research teams, we build geospatial analytics that improve forecasting accuracy and reduce uncertainty in high-stakes land and climate decisions.

Who we support

Conservation programs, infrastructure planners, and scientific teams working with spatially complex datasets.

Typical outcomes

Improved predictive accuracy, faster scenario evaluation, and clearer communication of geospatial patterns.

Engagement model

Co-design objectives, build decision-ready models, and deliver reproducible workflows for internal teams.

What is Geospatial Modeling?

We combine remote sensing, GIS, and predictive modeling to quantify environmental and land-use dynamics across space and time.

Geospatial Modeling in Action

Our workflows produce map products and forecasts that teams can use for permitting, planning, and climate-risk communication.

Environmental Applications

  • Simulating invasive species spread and testing management strategies
  • Estimating forest carbon biomass using satellite imagery
  • Modeling ecosystem vulnerability under climate change

Urban Planning Applications

  • Assessing urban development’s impact on coastal water resources
  • Analyzing land use effects on erosion and sediment transport
