Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how we work, what we offer, or who we serve? You're in the right place.

What industries does QSC serve?

QSC works with clients in environmental science, manufacturing, public health, agriculture, and more. We build science-driven data and AI tools to solve real-world problems.

How does QSC ensure scientific accuracy?

Our team is composed of PhD-trained scientists and experienced engineers who prioritize rigor, transparency, and peer-reviewed methods in every project.

Can QSC build custom software or dashboards?

Yes, we frequently build custom web tools, APIs, dashboards, and machine learning pipelines tailored to each client's needs.

Where is QSC based?

We are headquartered in Michigan’s Upper Peninsula but work with partners across North America and globally.

What is QSC's typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on scope, but most projects range from 2 weeks to 6 months. We offer rapid prototyping and long-term collaboration options.
